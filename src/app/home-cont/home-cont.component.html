this month's recommended city: new york, usa

New York City, where towering skyscrapers meet vibrant neighborhoods and endless cultural experiences. Explore iconic landmarks like the Empire State Building and One World Trade Center, then lose yourself in the diverse communities of Little Italy, Chinatown, and Harlem. Take a leisurely stroll through Central Park, indulge in world-class cuisine, and immerse yourself in the arts scene with Broadway shows and renowned museums.

  • The Metropolitan Museum of Art

  • 9/11 Memorial and Museum
  • The Museum of Modern Art (MoMA)
